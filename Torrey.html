Site Requirements

This is a P3 site and USHPA mebership is required

Launch

Grass launch on cliff, must obey window rules concerning RC Airplanes. One pilot in the window. Announce launch and direction

Landing

Almost everyone top lands this site, on the grass, but iff the wind is light OR the pilot is not comfortable top landing, the beach presents a very safe option. Just be careful for the volleyball courts and the steel poles that line the area just below launch
