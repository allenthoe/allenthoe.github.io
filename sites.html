Soboba

Located in San Jacinto, this site can be thermic but also a very reliable ridge soaring site

Marshall

The most consistent site in SoCal (and maybe Earth?) and is a great place for peoiple who like to get high and go XC.

Mentone

When it's blowing north due to Santa Ana Conditions this site can often be a reliable back-up to Marshall and Soboba.

Pisgha Peak

Yucaipa, Ca. Can often be flown in winter, similar to Mentone.

Winchester Bowl

If it's too light at Soboba to Soar, odds are it will be stronger at Winchester bowl about 15 minutes away

Crestline

Like Marshall, pilots who don't top land can land at Andy Jackson Airpark

Torrey Pines

Located in San Diego, this site hosts 13 miles of the most gorgeous cliffs to soar over

Elsinore

One of the few North-East facing launches, this site can typically be a great convergence site with record altitudes

Blossom

Similar to Soboba in that it can be thermic but also a good ridge soaring site, this site requires the ability to top land
