Site Requirements

This is and ADVANCED site but NO ACTUAL Club governs this site

Launch

This site faces East and works in an East or North wind. There are 2 main launches, both require advanced kiting skills and terrain avoidance

Landing

The landing area is fenced off but has a hole in it (almost always). Be sure to keep speed as thermals continue to pop off all the way to the ground.
