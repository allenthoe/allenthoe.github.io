Basic Info

  • Not a USHPA site
  • Located off Greenspot road, east of Highland off the 210 freeway
  • Great spot in winter when it's blowing north at Marshall (Santa Ana Conditions)
  • BLM land, open to public, driveable dirt road
  • RLF - Powerlines present a hazard
  • Club afficliation - None

LZ

The LZ is located off greenspot road. It's surrounded by power lines, presenting a challenge for pilots that struggle with spot landings.
